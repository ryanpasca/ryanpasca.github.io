Ryan Pasca

Ryan Pasca is the founder of Farm to Nest, a platform that helps local farmers connect with geographically proximate consumers.
The Farm to Nest venture is a small piece of a larger puzzle to improve the quality of our food as well as giving our communities a better understanding of how we can best source our food.
Ryan also works full time in an account management and customer service type job where he helps businesses optimize their utilization of financial cloud software.
Ryan's passion for promoting and selling his ideas to like-minded individuals combined with his background in Chemistry and Software-as-a-Service has been the foundation for him finding his life's purpose.
Ryan's long term vision is to build a larger endeavor at the interface of health, nutrition, and technology to promote human longevity, preventative medicine, and overall quality of life.
In his spare time, Ryan enjoys reading, running, swimming, and watching UNC Basketball.
